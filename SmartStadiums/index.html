About Smart Stadiums

Smart Stadiums is a platform that helps stadium owners to provide their services to users who want to make reservations at the stadium to play matches against their friends with full management of the content in the mobile app and the reservations that users make. Mobile App provides many services to users:
- Register and log in and validate the account with OTP via SMS.
- Show all the stadiums that can the user make a reservation in it.
- Create a football team and add their friends to this team.
- Join an existing team.
- Create a match between their team and another team.
- Manage the reservation that the user makes throw his account.
