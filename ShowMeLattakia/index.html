About Show Me Lattakia

About Lattakia is an iOS app that helps you to learn more about my city Lattakia the app will show you ancient cities and castles,
and the best hotels and restaurants all that with a map layout that helps you to navigate to any location you want easily.

My role

This is a self-project, an idea I try to make real and I am working on that at this time, so I am the project manager, system analyser, backend developer and iOS developer.

Tools I used

- PBMN: so I have a good vision of my goals and services.
- UML: to develop a staple and scalable software
- Strapi.io: develop the backend, design and implement the APIs to provide the services that I want
- Postman: check and test APIs
- XCode: develop the iOS app

Thank you
Behind this project there is an interesting story, everything starts with a call, my friend who lives in Damascus the capital of Syria will come to Lattakia to have a vacation, he asked me if I can take him to special places in the town and helps him throw this vacation, Unfortunately  I can’t because of my work. However, he came to Lattakia and had a nice vacation, especially since Lattakia has one of the most beautiful nature and beaches in Syria. Then I realised how many people want to come and see my town, especially those who are not live in Syria, and how it is hard to know the right places to visit without paying too much to tourists company. And there is a thing you need to know, google Maps does not work probably in Syria for political reasons I do not know. So I decided to develop a simple app that let you see Lattakia and learn more about it to make this app scalable so I can add more locations and even more cities I developed a backend and dashboard using strapi.io to provide the information I want to the app via APIs.
